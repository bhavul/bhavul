### Hi there 👋

My name is Bhavul. I'm a Senior Research Engineer currently working for [Meta AI](https://meta.com/) on Agents Research. In recent past, I was supporting fine-tuning Large Language Models and Latent Diffusion Models to build innovative new products for Ads. Before GenAI, I was building foundational models to understand users for improving Reels and Video recommendations. Prior to Meta, I worked as a Research Scientist (ML Tech Lead) at [Zapr](https://www.zapr.in) driving company's Applied NLP Research efforts, and also their Recommendation System projects.

I have supported and engaged in independent AI research too. I was a core researcher with [Manifold RG](https://manifoldcomputing.com/) in 2023-2024. Feel free to reach out for collaboration, we are living in very interesting times, and I'll be happy to contribute to a vision for positive future with AI. I also enjoy building products, automating things and so I try to maintain some grip on full stack knowledge for the same. I'm an advocate for Open Source and "AI for Good", and enjoy giving talks at conferences or tech meetups.
